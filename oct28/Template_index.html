About the Action

This October 28th MEChA will be sponsoring MY COUNTRY, MY VOTE, a rally that will commence a nationwide voter registration drive as well as express a vociferous rejection of the anti-immigrant rhetoric that has dominated this electoral cycle. With like Dolores Huerta, Lt. Governor Joe Garcia, Federico Peña, and more, MEChA will be proud to sponsor an event that takes a stand for immigrant rights at the same time that it promotes civic engagement. Aside from music and speakers, we will also have radio personalities Erazno y La Chokolata broadcasting live from the University of Colorado-Boulder.

On this date MEChA will manifest our repudiation of the dehumanizing speech employed against communities of color in today’s political discourse. We will demonstrate our rejection of the dismissive and derogatory manner in which our cultures and identities are treated in both mainstream and extremist political discourse.

Presented with a social, political, and economic conditions adverse to our full exercise of our human rights and our ample enjoyment of a life with dignity and justice, we declare the following, speaking not only as Latin@s, but as people of color in solidarity with Black communities, Indigenous communities, and others:

1. WE REJECT the dehumanization and colonization of our People

2. WE REJECT the criminalization of our Communities

3. WE REJECT the exploitation of all Workers

4. WE DEMAND the respect of our educational rights regardless of socioeconomic or citizenship status

5. WE DEMAND the full respect of our human rights in our workplace, our schools, and our community

6. WE DEMAND ample guarantees for the respect and preservation of OUR HUMAN DIGNITY

FOR A LIFE WITH JUSTICE AND DIGNITY! UNITY MAKES STRENGTH! MOVIMIENTO ESTUDIANTIL CHICAN@ DE AZTLÁN
